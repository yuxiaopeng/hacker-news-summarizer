## 1. Meta的法律团队背弃了其伦理职责。

**原文标题**: Meta's Legal Team Abandoned Its Ethical Duties

**原文链接**: [https://www.afterbabel.com/p/how-metas-lawyers-perfected-the-playbook](https://www.afterbabel.com/p/how-metas-lawyers-perfected-the-playbook)

In this article, Casey Mock argues that Meta’s legal team has abandoned the foundational ethical standards of the American legal profession—balancing loyalty to the client with duties to the court and the country. Drawing a direct parallel to the "Big Tobacco" playbook of the 1970s, Mock asserts that Meta’s attorneys have prioritized corporate profit over public safety, specifically regarding the welfare of children.

The author details several instances of alleged misconduct:
*   **Suppression of Research:** Meta’s "Project Mercury" found that reduced usage decreased teen depression and anxiety, yet lawyers reportedly buried these findings.
*   **Evidence Destruction:** Whistleblowers Jason Sattizahn and Kayce Savage testified that legal counsel ordered the destruction of evidence concerning child exploitation and pedophile rings within Meta’s VR ecosystem. 
*   **Institutional Manipulation:** Lawyers reportedly sanitized research language, avoiding terms like "illegal," and maintained a "17-strike policy" for sex trafficking accounts before removal.

A critical turning point occurred in October 2025, when Judge Yvonne Williams invoked the "crime-fraud exception" to pierce Meta’s attorney-client privilege. She found probable cause that lawyers coached researchers to hide data on teen mental health harms to shield the company from liability.

Mock calls for aggressive accountability to prevent the "transformation of law into a service available only to those wealthy enough to corrupt it." He urges state bar associations to investigate senior leaders like Jennifer Newstead and Joel Kaplan for potential disbarment and suggests that junior lawyers complicit in these cover-ups should be deemed unhireable. Finally, he advocates for legislative reform to prevent corporations from using attorney-client privilege as a shield for systemic wrongdoing. He concludes that the integrity of the legal system depends on ensuring that lawyers cannot facilitate harm with impunity.

核心观点包括：

*   **美元的武器化：** 美国利用金融制裁（尤其是针对俄罗斯的制裁）引发了多国的警觉。通过冻结外汇储备并切断部分国家与 SWIFT 系统的联系，美国表明美元已成为一种政治工具，促使其他国家寻求替代方案以降低“制裁风险”。

*   **美国经济的不稳定性：** 美国国债的攀升和持续的通货膨胀正在削弱全球对美元长期价值的信心。因此，各国央行正通过减持美债并大幅增加黄金储备来优化其多元化投资组合。

**结论：**
尽管美元目前仍是主要的交易媒介，但本文认为其衰落不可避免。世界正向多元化的金融格局过渡，没有任何单一货币能拥有绝对权力，这反映了全球经济影响力格局的深刻转变。

## 3. Unix 管道卡牌游戏

**原文标题**: The Unix Pipe Card Game

**原文链接**: [https://punkx.org/unix-pipe-game/](https://punkx.org/unix-pipe-game/)

**Unix 管道卡牌游戏**是一款旨在教授儿童（及初学者）如何利用管道组合 Unix 命令以处理数据的教学工具。该游戏由作者 "jackdoe" 开发，重点在于掌握 `cat`、`grep`、`tail`、`head`、`wc`、`sort` 和 `uniq` 等基础命令。

**玩法与规则**
游戏开始时，玩家从提供的任务列表中选择一个目标，并确定是以构建最短还是最长的有效管道链作为获胜标准。玩家轮流从牌堆中抽卡，组建能够完成目标的命令序列。任务示例包括：
*   打印特定行（例如第 7 行或倒数第 2 行）。
*   找出文件中出现频率最高或最低的行。
*   统计包含特定字符串或字符的行数。
*   构建一个不产生输出的命令链。

**教学方式**
游戏鼓励家长在实时终端或 jslinux 等基于浏览器的模拟器上演示这些命令。对于进阶玩家，游戏还提供了一个扩展包，引入了进程替换以及 `paste`、`tr`、`cut` 和 `bc` 等附加工具。

**获取方式与相关项目**
该游戏采用 CC BY 4.0 许可协议，提供成品购买或可供自行打印的 PDF 版本。作者还为儿童开发了一系列其他技术类游戏，涵盖 Python 编程、C 指针、机器码 (4917)、RISC-V 汇编 (PROJEKT: OVERFLOW) 以及游程编码等主题。

## 4. 截至2025年的IP地址

**原文标题**: IP Addresses Through 2025

**原文链接**: [https://www.potaroo.net/ispcol/2026-01/addr2025.html](https://www.potaroo.net/ispcol/2026-01/addr2025.html)

In "IP Addresses Through 2025," Geoff Huston reviews the state of internet addressing from the perspective of January 2026. Despite 1990s predictions that IPv4’s 4-billion-address limit would necessitate a rapid shift to IPv6, IPv4 remains dominant. This persistence is primarily due to the transition from peer-to-peer to client-server architectures and the widespread use of Network Address Translation (NAT), which allows over 30 billion devices to share roughly 3 billion public IPv4 addresses.

Huston warns that NAT is not a permanent solution. Over-reliance on it may eventually fragment the internet into disconnected "service cones" based on content distribution boundaries. While IPv6 was designed to provide a cohesive, universal address space, its deployment remains slow, lacking a common sense of urgency among operators.

Key data from 2025 includes:
*   **Contraction:** The total allocated IPv4 pool shrunk by approximately 237,000 addresses (0.01%), ending the year at 3.687 billion.
*   **Depletion:** The IANA pool has been exhausted for years. Most Regional Internet Registries (RIRs) are now managing residual "Available" and "Reserved" pools, with the largest remaining quantities held by APNIC and AFRINIC.
*   **Class E Space:** Roughly 268 million addresses remain reserved for "Future Use." However, efforts to release this space for general use have stalled due to implementation hurdles and the consensus that resources are better spent on IPv6 transition.

Ultimately, the article suggests that the internet is at a crossroads. It will either achieve a cohesive whole through increased IPv6 adoption or face a future of protocol fragmentation as IPv4 reaches its absolute limits.
