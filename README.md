## 1. 西非发现一处繁茂的珊瑚礁，此前长期被认为已死亡。

**原文标题**: Long presumed dead, a thriving coral reef is discovered in West Africa

**原文链接**: [https://e360.yale.edu/digest/benin-coral-reef](https://e360.yale.edu/digest/benin-coral-reef)

时隔60年，一支贝宁科学家团队在西非贝宁沿海重新发现了一片生机勃勃的珊瑚礁。该珊瑚礁最初记载于20世纪60年代的一份简短报告中，随后便被遗忘；由于全球珊瑚白化和过度捕捞，研究人员长期以来一直认为其已经死亡。

此次重新发现由“贝宁珊瑚礁重新发现与探索项目”负责人热拉尔·津津多胡埃（Gérard Zinzindohoué）领导。在国家地理探险家基金2万美元的资助下，该团队克服了缺乏固定研究船和设备采购困难等重大物流障碍。通过在当地渔船上部署声呐和高分辨率深海摄像机，他们在距离海岸约14英里、深度超过175英尺的海域找到了这片珊瑚礁。

该遗址被归类为中光层珊瑚生态系统（MCE），这是一种存在于造礁珊瑚生长深度下限的光依赖型生物群落。调查显示，这是一个健康的生态系统，拥有包括软珊瑚和黑珊瑚在内的8种珊瑚，以及非洲金鲷和几内亚刺盖鱼等8种鱼类。

除了丰富的生物多样性，这片珊瑚礁还具有巨大的科学和保护价值。研究人员认为，通过碳定年法，它可以提供关键的古气候数据，帮助解释过去的气候系统。此外，由于该区域被怀疑是濒危鲨鱼和鳐鱼的栖息地，保护主义者目前正倡议将其设立为海洋保护区。

这一发现是西非海洋科学的一个里程碑。津津多胡埃及其同事希望这一成功将激发几内亚湾新一轮由当地主导的研究，并强调非洲国家必须承担起探索和保护本国沿海环境的责任。

## 2. Gemini 3.6 Flash、3.5 Flash-Lite 以及 3.5 Flash Cyber

**原文标题**: Gemini 3.6 Flash, 3.5 Flash-Lite, and 3.5 Flash Cyber

**原文链接**: [https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/models-and-research/gemini-models/gemini-3-6-flash-3-5-flash-lite-3-5-flash-cyber/](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/models-and-research/gemini-models/gemini-3-6-flash-3-5-flash-lite-3-5-flash-cyber/)

Google’s July 21, 2026, announcement introduces three new models in the Gemini series—**3.6 Flash, 3.5 Flash-Lite, and 3.5 Flash Cyber**—specifically designed to enhance the efficiency, speed, and reliability of AI agentic workflows.

*   **Gemini 3.6 Flash:** Positioned as the "workhorse" model, it offers a significant step up in coding, knowledge work, and multimodal tasks. It is more token-efficient than 3.5 Flash, reducing output token usage by 17% to 65% depending on the benchmark. It is priced lower at $1.50 per 1M input tokens and $7.50 per 1M output tokens, featuring enhanced safety safeguards against CBRN and cyber misuse.
*   **Gemini 3.5 Flash-Lite:** This is the fastest and most cost-effective model in the 3.5 series, delivering 350 output tokens per second. Priced at $0.30 per 1M input tokens, it is optimized for high-throughput tasks like document processing and agentic search. It notably outperforms previous generations and even the standard Gemini 3 in specific coding and real-world execution benchmarks.
*   **Gemini 3.5 Flash Cyber:** Integrated into the **CodeMender** agent, this specialized model is fine-tuned to detect and patch cybersecurity vulnerabilities. To prevent misuse, it is currently restricted to a limited-access pilot program for governments and trusted partners.

**Future Outlook and Availability:**
Google also revealed that **Gemini 3.5 Pro** is in testing with partners and that pre-training for **Gemini 4** has officially begun. Gemini 3.6 Flash and 3.5 Flash-Lite are available immediately via the Gemini API, Google AI Studio, and the Gemini app, with 3.5 Flash-Lite also rolling out in Google Search.
