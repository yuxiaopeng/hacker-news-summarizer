## 6. 欢迎来到废土：千座汽油镇

**原文标题**: Welcome to the Wasteland: A Thousand Gas Towns

**原文链接**: [https://steve-yegge.medium.com/welcome-to-the-wasteland-a-thousand-gas-towns-a5eb9bc8dc1f](https://steve-yegge.medium.com/welcome-to-the-wasteland-a-thousand-gas-towns-a5eb9bc8dc1f)

在《欢迎来到荒原：一千个汽油镇》一文中，史蒂夫·耶基（Steve Yegge）探讨了大语言模型（LLM）对软件行业的颠覆性影响。他借用《疯狂的麦克斯》中的隐喻，将当前的科技版图描述为一片“荒原”，而那些被他称为“汽油镇”的传统 SaaS 公司，正面临商业模式被 AI 彻底淘汰的困境。

其核心前提是，LLM 从根本上瓦解了软件开发的成本和难度。耶基认为，“传统 SaaS”所提供的大部分价值正在被商品化，因为 AI 现在只需极低的成本和极短的时间，就能复制出复杂的功能。那些依赖于专有 UI 或基础数据处理等简单“护城河”的公司，极易被能够按需生成相同结果的 AI 原生解决方案所取代。

耶基指出了几个关键转变：
1. **“码农”的消亡：** 手动编程的价值正在缩水。软件工程师必须进化为高水平的架构师和 AI 编排者，负责管理 AI 智能体，而不是亲手编写每一行代码。
2. **LLM 原生开发：** 生存的关键在于构建以 AI 为核心引擎的产品，而不仅仅是将其作为插件功能。他以 Sourcegraph 及其 AI 助手 Cody 的进化为例，说明了这种转型。
3. **速度是唯一的护城河：** 在一个 AI 可以瞬间构建软件的世界里，唯一的竞争方式就是通过快速迭代，并解决 AI 尚无法自主处理的高复杂度问题。

最后，耶基警告说，我们正处于 AI 的“寒武纪大爆发”之中。虽然这场转型充满混乱，且对老牌企业构成了威胁，但对于那些能够通过拥抱 AI 驱动开发来穿越“荒原”、构建下一代软件的人来说，这预示着巨大的机遇。

## 7. Raycast 出品的 Glaze

**原文标题**: Glaze by Raycast

**原文链接**: [https://www.glazeapp.com/](https://www.glazeapp.com/)

**Glaze by Raycast** is an AI-powered platform designed to revolutionize desktop app development by allowing users to create custom applications through simple chat interactions. Unlike web-based AI builders such as v0 or Replit, Glaze focuses specifically on native desktop environments, enabling apps to access local files, hardware (like cameras), keyboard shortcuts, and menu bar integrations.

**Key Features:**
*   **No-Code Creation:** Users describe their desired app in plain language, and Glaze builds it. It caters to both non-coders and developers who wish to further refine the code.
*   **Local-First & OS-Integrated:** Apps run directly on the user's machine without requiring a server or internet connection, allowing for deep integration with the operating system.
*   **Team Collaboration:** Teams can build internal tools, share them via private stores, and integrate them with existing APIs and processes.
*   **Design-Oriented:** The platform is "opinionated," meaning it automatically applies a high standard of design to ensure apps are beautiful and professional by default.

**Availability and Pricing:**
Glaze is currently in private beta for Mac, with Windows and Linux support planned for the future. While Raycast users and event attendees receive priority access, others can join a waitlist. The service will operate on a freemium model: a free tier provides daily building credits, while a paid plan ($20/month) offers a larger credit bundle and private stores for teams. 

In summary, Glaze aims to democratize desktop software creation, moving beyond the limitations of web apps to provide powerful, locally-integrated tools for personal and professional use.
